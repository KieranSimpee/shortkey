Video & Poster Tools
Generation workflows for HeyGen, Runway, and Kling · Exact inputs required before Kura can generate
Poster — Required Inputs
Product Image
High-res PNG, white or transparent bg. Min 1000x1000px. One product per image. No lifestyle photography for poster use.
Required before generation
Campaign Headline
Max 6 words. No punctuation. Bold and clear. Example: "Summer Glow Live Tonight". Must fit 2 lines at max font size.
Required before generation
Theme & Format
Theme: Main / Skincare / Makeup. Format: Landscape 1200x627px, Portrait 1080x1920px, or Square 1080x1080px.
Required before generation
Call to Action
Single action, max 4 words. Examples: "Watch Live Now", "Shop Skincare", "Try On Today". Used for ghost CTA button text.
Required before generation
Video Tools
HG
HeyGen
AI Avatar · Voiceover · Talking Head
Brand intro video with AI spokesperson (shortkey.info portal)
Multilingual voiceover — EN, CN, HK, KR
Talking-head product explainer with on-screen product
Inputs Kura Needs
Script — max 300 words per minute of video
Avatar selection or brand photo for custom avatar
Language — EN / CN / HK / KR
Brand logo PNG transparent for watermark
Background — brand theme colour or image
Check HeyGen credits before batch generation. Long videos consume more.
RW
Runway
Image-to-Video · Cinematic Motion
Product beauty shots animated into cinematic video (bottle, texture, pour)
Live stream intro animation for shortkey.live
TikTok-style beauty reel from still product images
Inputs Kura Needs
Product image — high-res PNG, white or natural bg
Motion prompt — e.g. "slow zoom into bottle, soft diffused lighting"
Duration — 4s or 8s per Gen-3 clip
Aspect ratio — 16:9 landscape or 9:16 portrait
Gen-3 credits per month. Each 4s clip = approx 1 credit. Check dashboard before batch runs.
KL
Kling AI
Character Motion · Lip Sync · Tutorial
Beauty tutorial — model applying makeup, natural motion
Lip sync for beauty reviews from influencer photo
Before/after skin transformation for skincare category
Inputs Kura Needs
Reference image — model face or product photo
Motion prompt — e.g. "applies lip tint, smiles to camera, soft lighting"
Audio file for lip sync (if voiceover required)
Resolution — 720p or 1080p
Professional plan recommended for 1080p. Check remaining credits in Kling dashboard.
