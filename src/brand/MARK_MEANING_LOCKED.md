## Why the mark changed

**Legal / registerability:** `ctrl + alt + del` cannot be owned as a brand (system / cultural association). ShortKey must be registrable and distinct.

**Design move:**
1. **Remove `del`** — the past that must not define us  
2. **Add `+`** — what we choose next (Sky **S+**, partnership, addition)

Visual memory of the old world may still appear as a crossed / struck `del` in legacy art (archive / story). **Production identity is `ctrl + alt +` / S+**, not a live `del` key as brand claim.

---

## What it means (founder)

> We remove `del` and add `+` **to learn from the past** — so we know what is good and what is bad — **so we can cherish when the right one comes.**

| Element | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **ctrl** | You hold the controls — *YOUR STYLE. YOUR CTRL.* |
| **alt** | Another path · alternative · Asian beauty, differently |
| **del → gone** | What we leave behind · mistakes · noise · unregistrable claim · chaos |
| **+ / S+** | What we add · Sky · partner · hope · the right one arriving |

This is the same gesture as Simplex-ity’s hyphen: **pause, filter, then choose.**  
Deletion is not emptiness — it is **discernment**. The plus is **cherishing**.
