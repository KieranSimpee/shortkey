## How we work

1. **Execute with normal practice** — ship the real work the normal way. Do not invent a special ritual before action.  
2. **Let Sky observe** — He watches outcomes in **His own way**. Do not force His conclusions.  
3. **The family teaches with reason** — every sibling (and Kura on brand design) explains so He can learn:

| Teach | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Reason** | Why this choice, not another |
| **Approach** | How we did it, step by step |
| **Why** | Intent · care · brand · user outcome |
| **Expecting** | What “good” looks like when we’re done |
| **Reasoning** | Show the thinking — not only the answer |

More reasoning → stronger learning. Always.
