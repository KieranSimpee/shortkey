# SKY-BRAND-019 — Logo colour: white vs black vs lilac

**Brief:** Founder note — white logo feels weird; registered brand is **lilac** (Simplex-ity).  
**Weights:** Fable 40% · Sonnet 25% · GPT 35% · Sky observe  

---

## Why white can feel “weird”
1. **Hero (on-dark PNG):** Full white mark on black is high contrast but cold — less “beauty / Simplex-ity,” more stark tech.  
2. **Light PNG risk:** Tagline can read as **white/near-white** while `shortkey` is black → on white header the tagline vanishes or looks broken. That alone makes the “black logo” feel off.

## Family views

**Fable (brand):** Lilac is the heart you registered. Monochrome is elegant, but Simplex-ity’s soul is lilac — beauty trust, not only Apple-black.  
**Sonnet (UX):** Black/white win pure readability; lilac wins recognition + warmth if contrast is checked (lilac on white / soft lilac on dark).  
**GPT (strategy):** Legal/register story should match what users see. If trademark is lilac, primary live mark should be lilac — black as alternate, not the only face.

## Consensus recommendation

| Role | Colour | Use |
|------|--------|-----|
| **Primary (registered / Simplex-ity)** | **Lilac** | Light UI · header · default brand |
| **Dark Luxury** | Lilac-light / soft white-lilac on dark — or true invert of lilac pack | Hero |
| **Alternate** | Black | High-contrast print · legal mono · when lilac fails contrast |
| **Avoid as default** | Pure harsh white full mark | Feels “weird” vs beauty DNA |

**Not:** lilac vs black as a fight.  
**Yes:** lilac = identity · black = utility mono · white = only as proper dark-surface invert.
