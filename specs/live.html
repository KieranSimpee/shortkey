How It Works
👤
For Customers
How to watch, try, buy, and claim your VIP slot
1
Join shortkey.live free
Create your free account below. You need a membership to claim VIP slots and receive your personal Try-On photo.
Join Free — scroll to bottom
2
Check the public schedule
See Who's Live Now and upcoming shows on the calendar. Tap Save Date to download a .ics file — it opens in Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook automatically.
Calendar tab above
3
Try-On before you buy
Click Quick Try-On on any product or calendar card. The TINT AI engine lets you see every shade on your own face — no app download needed.
shortkey.fashion/try-on
4
Watch the live show
Click Watch Live when the show starts. The influencer demos every product live. Ask questions in the live chat.
Watch Now nav link
5
Buy the complete set — claim VIP
During the show, purchase all featured products from tonight's show. If you are one of the first 10 to complete the full set, you qualify for the VIP slot.
Buy Set — Claim Slot button
6
Receive your exclusive photo
After the show ends, the system generates your personal Try-On photo — your face, the full look, side-by-side with the influencer. Delivered to your account. One tap to share on Instagram or TikTok.
VIP slots are strictly first-come-first-served. Once all 10 slots are filled, the show closes to new VIP claims. Join free to get real-time slot alerts.
🎥
For Influencers & Brands
How to submit, get approved, and go live
1
Brand must be onboarded
Only brands with an active Vendor Number (SK-B-XXX) can submit show requests. If your brand is not yet onboarded, go to shortkey.info to apply.
shortkey.info/onboard
2
Submit your show request
Click the "Brand & Influencer Submissions" tab above. Fill in your brand name, vendor number, influencer handle, preferred date/time, show title, and products to feature.
Submissions tab above
3
Wait for Wednesday review
Kieran reviews all submissions every Wednesday. Approved shows are confirmed and automatically added to the public Who's Live calendar. You receive confirmation by email within 48 hours.
4
Confirm products are stocked
Before your show date, confirm that all featured products have at least 500 units in stock and your DHL account is active. Minimum 7-day shipping commitment to customers.
5
Go live on shortkey.live
On show day, the Shortkey team sets up the Vimeo stream. The influencer connects and goes live on shortkey.live. TINT Try-On syncs automatically with your featured products — viewers can try everything without leaving the stream.
6
Commission settled in 45 days
Revenue is tracked per show. Commission is calculated after the 45-day payment term — allowing time for influencer commissions and returns to be reconciled before paying out to your brand.
Platform rate: 5–15% (tier-based)
Show slots are limited. Submit early — popular dates fill up. Collaborating with other brands on one show increases audience and is encouraged.
